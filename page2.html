Example:

T: R2 → R2

T(x, y) = (x, 2x); x, y ∈ R2

1. Null Space ≡ N(T) = {(0, y) : y ∈ R}

 Nullity = dim N(T) = 1

2. Range ≡ R(T) = {(x, 2x) : x ∈ R}

 Rank T = dim R(T) = 1
